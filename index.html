Extended Bio

My career thus far is a story of two chapters. The first is working in variety of customer-facing and operational roles in fast growth startups. The second (and current) chapter is as a software engineer. I graduated from Georgetown University's School of Foreign Service in 2014 with a bachelor's degree in Science, Technology, and International Affairs. During college I studied abroad for a year in Buenos Aires, Argentina, which sparked a lifelong interest in South American history, politics, languages, and economy.

During my last year of college I committed myself to finding an opportunity to return to South America - this time Brazil - and ultimately connected with then-fledgling data analytics startup, Neoway. Through a combination of exceptional Brazilian hospitality and blind luck, I completed a ~5 month internship at Neoway in São Paulo, Brazil. After this internship I returned to Washington, DC and began working with Social Tables in a variety of Customer Success roles. After approximately three years with Social Tables I moved to New York to join Neoway once again, this time leading Neoway's international expansion efforts into the US market.

In my Customer Success roles, I frequently collaborated with product managers, software engineers, and data scientists at every level and ultimately in 2019 decided to shift my career in a more technical direction actually building products for market. I enrolled in App Academy, a full-time 4 month immersive coding school, to focus full-time on building sufficient technical expertise to contribute to software projects myself.

When COVID-19 swept across the world, I was living in New York City. I wanted to be a part of the solution as much as possible and worked part-time for Covid Watch, an open source nonprofit founded in February 2020 pioneering a decentralized, privacy-preserving exposure notification system for people to understand their level of exposure to COVID-19. You can find that Github repository here. I've been quoted in the Wall Street Journal discussing this landscape. Covid Watch is the first organization in the United States to roll out an app using the Google/Apple exposure notification API and dynamic risk model. Read more about Covid Watch here.

Currently, I'm working as a Software Engineer at Tesla.
